Privacy Policy

The Teacher's Satchel — Android Application
Last updated: February 15, 2026

Introduction

This privacy policy describes how the application "The Teacher's Satchel" (hereinafter "the App") handles its users' data. The App is published by Graya.

The Teacher's Satchel is a tool designed for teachers. It operates primarily offline and has been built to minimize data collection.

Data stored locally

The App stores certain data directly on your device (local storage) in order to function properly. This includes:

  • Your preferences and settings
  • Files and content you create within the App

This data remains on your device. It is neither transmitted to a remote server nor accessible by the App's publisher. You can delete it at any time by clearing the App's data or uninstalling it.

Automatically collected data (crash reports)

The App uses Firebase Crashlytics, a service provided by Google, to detect and fix technical errors. In the event of a crash or error, Crashlytics may automatically collect the following information:

  • Device information: model, manufacturer, screen orientation
  • Operating system and its version
  • Available memory and disk space at the time of the crash
  • Stack trace of the error
  • App version

This data is transmitted to Firebase (Google) and is used exclusively to improve the App's stability. It does not contain any personally identifiable information (name, email, location, etc.) and is not cross-referenced with other services.

To learn more about how Firebase processes data, please refer to the Firebase privacy policy.

Data not collected

The App does not collect or process any of the following data:

  • Personal identification data (name, email address, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, or files stored on your device
  • Usage data for advertising purposes
  • Payment or financial data

Data sharing with third parties

No data is sold, traded, or transmitted to third parties for commercial or advertising purposes. The only data shared with a third party (Google/Firebase) consists of the crash reports described above, solely for the purpose of improving the App.

Advertising

The App does not contain any advertising.

Target audience

The App is intended for teachers and education professionals. It is not designed to be used by children under the age of 13.

Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. The date of the last update is shown at the top of this page. We recommend reviewing this page periodically.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the handling of your data, you can contact the publisher at: contact@graya.fr
